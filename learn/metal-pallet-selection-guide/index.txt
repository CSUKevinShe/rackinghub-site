3:T3f57,<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">Why Metal Pallets?</h2>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Metal pallets — typically steel or galvanized steel — offer advantages that wood and plastic pallets cannot match in specific industrial applications. They support higher loads, last longer, and meet strict hygiene and fire safety requirements. This guide helps you determine when metal pallets are the right choice and how to specify them.</p>

<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">Types of Metal Pallets</h2>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Steel Pallets</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">The most common type, made from formed steel sheet or tube. Surface finishes:</p>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Powder-coated**: Color-coded, basic corrosion protection, indoor use</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Galvanized**: Hot-dip or electro-galvanized, excellent corrosion resistance</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Stainless steel**: Food/pharma grade, highest corrosion resistance</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Wire Mesh Pallets</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Lighter than solid steel pallets. Open deck allows for:</p>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Better ventilation (cold storage)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Easy cleaning (food/hygiene applications)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Water drainage (wet environments)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Fork and pallet jack access from all four sides</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Stackable Metal Pallets</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Designed for stacking without racking. Features interlocking posts or nesting feet for stable stacking 3-4 units high.</p>

<div class="overflow-x-auto my-4"><table class="w-full text-sm border border-slate-200 rounded-lg"><tbody><tr><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Rating</td><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Description</td></tr></tbody></table></div>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">|--------|-------------|</p>
<div class="overflow-x-auto my-4"><table class="w-full text-sm border border-slate-200 rounded-lg"><tbody><tr><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Static load</td><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Maximum weight when pallet is stationary (stacked or racked)</td></tr></tbody></table></div>
<div class="overflow-x-auto my-4"><table class="w-full text-sm border border-slate-200 rounded-lg"><tbody><tr><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Dynamic load</td><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Maximum weight when pallet is being moved by forklift</td></tr></tbody></table></div>
<div class="overflow-x-auto my-4"><table class="w-full text-sm border border-slate-200 rounded-lg"><tbody><tr><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Racking load</td><td class="px-3 py-2 border-b border-slate-100 text-slate-600">Maximum weight when supported on two beams across span</td></tr></tbody></table></div>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3"><strong class="text-slate-800">Important</strong>: Racking load is always lower than static load. A pallet rated 3,000 kg static may only support 1,500 kg on a rack. Always check the manufacturer's racking load rating.</p>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Fork Entry</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**2-way**: Fork pockets on two opposite sides</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**4-way**: Fork pockets on all four sides (requires recessed design)</li>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">Standard fork pocket dimensions: 180×75mm (L×H). Verify compatibility with your forklift and automated systems.</p>
<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">Application Guide</h2>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">When to Choose Metal Pallets</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Heavy loads** over 1,000 kg per pallet</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Food and pharmaceutical** environments requiring wash-down</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Clean rooms** and controlled environments</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Fire-rated** storage areas (metal pallets contribute no fuel load)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Long-term investment** — amortize over 10+ years</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Outdoor storage** where plastic degrades from UV exposure</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">When Plastic Is Better</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Lightweight handling is a priority</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Automated systems require precise dimensions (plastic molding is more consistent)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Non-corrosive environments</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">One-way shipping (disposable)</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">When Wood Is Better</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Lowest initial cost is the primary concern</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">One-way export shipments (ISPM 15 compliant)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">Easy customization needed on-site</li>
<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">Cost Considerations</h2>
