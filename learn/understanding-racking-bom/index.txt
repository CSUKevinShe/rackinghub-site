3:T1066,<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">What Is a Bill of Materials (BOM)?</h2>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">A BOM is a comprehensive list of every component needed for a racking installation. Accurate BOM preparation is critical for procurement, cost estimation, and project management.</p>
<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">Major Components</h2>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Structural Components</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Upright frames**: Complete assembled units (2 uprights + bracing)</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Beams**: Box beams in various lengths and profiles</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Bracing**: Horizontal and diagonal braces for upright frames</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Shims**: Steel plates for leveling on uneven floors</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Base plates**: For anchoring uprights to the floor</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Decking</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Wire mesh decks**: Sized to fit beam spans</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Pallet support bars**: Where mesh decks are not used</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Steel shelf panels**: For light-duty shelving applications</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Safety Accessories</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Column protectors**: Post guards for aisle-facing uprights</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Frame protectors**: Full-height upright guards</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Back-of-rack meshes**: Wire mesh panels for rear protection</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Load notices**: Capacity labels for every beam level</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Rack end barriers**: Safety barriers at row ends</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Pallet stoppers**: Prevent pallets from being pushed too deep</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Connection Hardware</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Beam locks/connectors**: Beam-to-upright connection clips</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Bolts and nuts**: Anchor bolts, row spacer bolts</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Row spacers**: Between back-to-back frames</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Anchor bolts**: For securing to concrete floor</li>
<h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-800 mt-6 mb-2">Installation Materials</h3>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Anchor epoxy**: Chemical anchors for cracked or low-strength concrete</li>
<li class="text-sm text-slate-600 ml-4 mb-1">**Floor marking paint**: Aisle and safety zone marking</li>
<h2 class="text-xl font-bold text-slate-900 mt-8 mb-3">BOM Preparation Tips</h2>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">1. <strong class="text-slate-800">Start with the layout</strong> — A clear 2D layout determines component counts</p>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">2. <strong class="text-slate-800">Count uprights carefully</strong> — Include corner and interior positions separately</p>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">3. <strong class="text-slate-800">Match beams to pallet positions</strong> — Each pallet position needs a beam pair</p>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">4. <strong class="text-slate-800">Add 5% contingency</strong> — For damages during installation</p>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">5. <strong class="text-slate-800">Include safety accessories</strong> — Never skip column protectors and load notices</p>
<p class="text-sm text-slate-600 leading-relaxed mb-3">6. <strong class="text-slate-800">Specify steel grade</strong> — S250, S300, or S355 significantly affects pricing</p>
