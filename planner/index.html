Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the RackingHub Planner

Is the planner really free?

+

Yes — 100% free, no registration or email required. Enter your warehouse specs and get instant racking recommendations, layout diagrams, and cost estimates. Use it as many times as you want.

What racking types does it support?

+

Currently we support Heavy-Duty Selective, Drive-In/Drive-Through, and Radio Shuttle racking. These cover 90% of warehouse use cases. Custom configurations can be discussed with our engineering team via the quote form.

How accurate are the cost estimates?

+

Cost estimates are based on current steel market rates and our standard pricing. They serve as a rough budget guide — actual prices may vary depending on steel market fluctuations, shipping costs, customization requirements, and order volume. Contact us for a detailed, binding quotation.

Can I customize pallet dimensions and rack specs?

+

Yes. You can adjust pallet size (width/depth/height), rack dimensions, number of levels, pallets per bay, aisle widths, wall clearances, and column spacing. The layout diagram updates in real-time as you change parameters.

Does the planner account for fire safety and building codes?

+

No — regulations vary by country and region. The planner provides a preliminary layout for planning purposes only. Always verify with a local structural engineer and fire safety consultant before finalizing your design.

What happens after I request a quote?

+

Our engineering team reviews your warehouse specs and recommended plan, then contacts you within 24 hours with a detailed quotation including CAD drawings, structural calculations, pricing breakdown, and delivery timeline. No obligation — just a professional assessment.
