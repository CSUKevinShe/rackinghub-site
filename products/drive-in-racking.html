Drive-In Racking

High-density storage for homogeneous products

Request Quote WhatsApp

Drive-in racking is a high-density storage system that allows forklifts to drive directly into the rack structure. Perfect for storing large quantities of homogeneous products with LIFO inventory management.

Key Features

  • High-density storage (up to 75% space utilization)
  • Forklift access directly into racks
  • LIFO inventory management
  • Ideal for bulk storage
  • Cost-effective solution
  • Customizable depth and height

Specifications

MaterialCold-rolled steel Q235
Load Capacity500-2000kg per pallet position
HeightUp to 12m
Depth2-10 pallets deep
Lane WidthCustomizable
SurfacePowder coating
ColorBlue, Orange, Grey (customizable)

Applications

  • Cold storage facilities
  • Beverage industry
  • Food and beverage distribution
  • Chemical industry
  • Large quantities of same SKU
