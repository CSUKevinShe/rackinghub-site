Warehouse Cages

Secure storage containers for organized warehousing

Request Quote WhatsApp

Warehouse cages, also known as wire mesh containers or stillage cages, provide secure and organized storage for parts, components, and materials. Stackable and foldable designs maximize space efficiency.

Key Features

  • Heavy-duty steel wire construction
  • Stackable design (2-4 levels)
  • Foldable for space-saving return shipping
  • Forklift pockets for easy handling
  • Optional wheels for mobility
  • Powder-coated finish for durability

Specifications

MaterialSteel wire Q235
Load Capacity500-2000kg
Size1200×1000×1000mm, 1000×800×800mm (customizable)
Wire Diameter5-8mm
Mesh Size50×50mm, 50×100mm
SurfacePowder coating / Galvanized
Stacking2-4 levels

Applications

  • Auto parts storage
  • Manufacturing work-in-progress
  • Warehouse organization
  • Recycling and waste management
  • Agricultural product storage
  • Retail backroom storage
