Plastic Pallets

Hygienic and durable pallets for modern logistics

Request Quote WhatsApp

Plastic pallets offer a hygienic, durable, and lightweight alternative to traditional wooden pallets. Ideal for food industry, pharmaceuticals, and export applications where cleanliness and pest-free materials are required.

Key Features

  • 100% recyclable HDPE/PP material
  • Hygienic and easy to clean
  • No nails, splinters, or pests
  • Moisture and chemical resistant
  • Lightweight yet durable
  • Long service life (5-10 years)

Specifications

MaterialHDPE / PP (Virgin or Recycled)
Load CapacityDynamic: 500-2000kg / Static: 2000-8000kg
Size1200×1000mm, 1100×1100mm, 1200×800mm (customizable)
Weight8-25kg (depending on model)
TypeSingle-faced / Double-faced
Entry2-way / 4-way
ColorBlue, Red, Green, Black (customizable)

Applications

  • Food and beverage industry
  • Pharmaceutical and healthcare
  • Export shipping (ISPM-15 exempt)
  • Cold storage facilities
  • Clean room environments
  • Automotive industry
