Industrial Plastic Pallets for Logistics & Warehousing

HDPE and PP reusable pallets engineered for high-load, hygienic, and export-ready operations

Request Quote WhatsApp

RackingHub supplies industrial plastic pallets manufactured by Boracs, our production facility in Nanjing operating since 2004. These HDPE pallets and PP pallets replace wooden alternatives across food processing, pharmaceuticals, cold chain, and export logistics. Injection-molded from virgin or recycled resin, reusable shipping pallets from our line deliver consistent dimensional accuracy, static load ratings up to 6000kg, and service life measured in years rather than months. Plastic warehouse pallets require no fumigation (ISPM-15 exempt), carry no splinter or nail hazards, and ship to 60+ countries under ISO9001:2015 and CE certification.

Why Specify Plastic Pallets

Key Features

  • Injection-molded HDPE or PP — 100% recyclable material with consistent wall thickness
  • Static load capacity up to 6000kg; dynamic load 1000-2000kg depending on model
  • Non-absorbent surface resists moisture, acids, alkalis, and solvents
  • Zero nails, zero splinters, zero pest risk — critical for food-grade and cleanroom use
  • Lightweight versus timber (8-25kg per unit) reduces freight cost
  • Service life of 5-10 years in normal warehouse rotation
  • Available in single-faced and double-faced decks; 2-way or 4-way entry
  • Anti-slip deck surface options and steel-reinforced inserts available for racking

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum load capacity of your industrial plastic pallets?

Our industrial plastic pallets handle a static load of up to 6000kg and a dynamic load of 1000-2000kg depending on the model. Heavy-duty reinforced designs with optional steel inserts are available for pallet racking applications where racking load matters most.

What is the difference between HDPE and PP pallets?

HDPE pallets offer higher impact resistance and perform reliably in cold storage environments down to -40°C. PP pallets provide greater stiffness and heat resistance, making them the right choice for hot-fill applications. We supply both materials and will recommend based on your specific operating temperature and load requirements.

Can you produce custom sizes and colors for plastic pallets?

Yes. Standard sizes include 1200×1000mm, 1100×1100mm, and 1200×800mm. We manufacture to custom dimensions on request. Color options include blue, red, green, and black, with custom colors available for large orders to support internal color-coding systems.

Are your plastic pallets suitable for food and pharmaceutical use?

Yes. We produce food-grade HDPE pallets that meet hygiene requirements for food processing and pharmaceutical handling. They are non-absorbent, easy to sanitize with standard cleaning agents, and free from nails or splinters that could contaminate products or damage packaging.

What certifications do your plastic pallets carry?

Our Nanjing facility operates under ISO9001:2015 quality management and CE certification. Products are exported to 60+ countries and comply with international shipping standards. All plastic pallets are ISPM-15 exempt, which eliminates fumigation requirements for cross-border shipments.
