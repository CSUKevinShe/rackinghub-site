Medium Duty Shelving Systems for Hand-Loading Storage

Engineered for warehouses, distribution centers, and fulfillment operations that need reliable carton storage without forklift access.

Request Quote WhatsApp

Medium duty shelving fills the gap between light-duty boltless units and pallet racking. When your team handles goods by hand — picking cartons, replenishing shelves, or organizing spare parts — a properly specified hand-loading shelving system saves time, reduces strain, and keeps your warehouse floor organized. Boracs manufactures these systems in our Nanjing plant using cold-rolled steel Q235B, with per-level capacities ranging from 200 to 800kg depending on the beam configuration you select.

Key Features

  • Load capacity of 200-800kg per shelf level, configured to match your actual weight requirements
  • Boltless beam-to-upright connection — shelf heights adjust in 50mm increments without tools
  • Full-span beam design eliminates sag under heavy carton loads
  • Electrostatic powder-coated finish resists scratches and corrosion in warehouse environments
  • Starter bays and add-on bays let you expand storage as your operation grows
  • Compatible with steel panel, wire mesh, particle board, and carton flow decking

Decking Options

The decking you choose directly affects how your team interacts with stored goods. We supply four options, each suited to different warehouse workflows:

  • Steel panel decking — solid surface for heavy cartons and boxed goods that need full support
  • Wire mesh decking — open grid allows light and air through, improves visibility and sprinkler coverage
  • Particle board decking — cost-effective for lighter items, electronics, and finished goods
  • Carton flow decking — gravity-fed rollers enable first-in-first-out picking for high-SKU fulfillment areas

Applications

Medium duty warehouse shelving works wherever goods move by hand rather than by pallet. Common installations include:

  • E-commerce fulfillment centers — carton storage for small and medium-sized SKUs
  • Automotive parts distribution — organized spare parts storage with bin dividers
  • Pharmaceutical and cold storage — wire mesh decking for temperature-controlled environments
  • Manufacturing replenishment zones — staging areas feeding assembly lines
  • Retail and 3PL backrooms — fast-picking storage for consumer goods and seasonal inventory

FAQ

What load capacity does medium duty shelving support?

Our medium duty shelving systems support 200-800kg per shelf level, depending on the beam profile and decking type selected. This makes them suitable for hand-loaded carton storage, spare parts, and small to medium-sized goods.

What is the difference between medium duty shelving and heavy duty racking?

Medium duty shelving is designed for manual, hand-loading operations with lighter goods stored in cartons or on shelves. Heavy duty racking uses pallets and requires forklifts, handling loads of 1,000-5,000kg per level. If your team picks items by hand, medium duty shelving is the right choice.

Can medium duty shelving be customized to fit my warehouse?

Yes. We manufacture medium duty shelving to your exact height, width, and depth requirements. Shelf levels are fully adjustable in 50mm increments. We also offer custom colors, decking types, and accessory options like dividers and label holders.

What decking options are available?

We offer four decking types: steel panel decking for heavy cartons, wire mesh decking for ventilation and visibility, particle board decking for lightweight items, and carton flow decking for FIFO picking operations. Each option is matched to your specific storage needs.

How long does it take to manufacture and deliver?

Standard configurations ship within 15-20 days from our Nanjing facility. Custom orders typically require 25-30 days. We have shipped to 60+ countries and handle export packaging, documentation, and port-side delivery. Contact us with your project timeline and we will give you an accurate lead time.
