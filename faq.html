🏗️ Technical & Standards

Q: What racking standards do you comply with?

A: Our racking systems comply with major international standards:

  • FEM 10.2.02 & 10.2.09 — European racking design standards
  • EN 15512 — European steel static racking
  • AS 4084:2012 — Australian standard
  • CE marking — Compliance certification

All designs include structural calculations per the applicable standard.

Q: Can you design a racking layout for my warehouse?

A: Yes. Send us your warehouse floor plan (CAD or PDF), and our engineering team will create a free layout drawing optimized for your space. We consider:

  • Forklift aisle widths
  • Pallet dimensions
  • Local seismic requirements

Q: What steel grade do you use for racking?

A: We use Q235B cold-rolled steel for uprights and beams. Quality controls include:

  • Tensile and yield strength testing on every batch
  • Powder coating thickness: 60–80μm, verified with adhesion square tests

🌍 International Shipping

Q: Do you ship to Europe? What are the incoterms?

A: Yes, we ship to all European ports regularly. We offer:

  • FOB — Ningbo/Shanghai
  • CIF — Destination port (most common for EU orders)
  • DDP — Door-to-door delivery

Transit time: 25–35 days to major EU ports.

Q: How do you pack racking for shipping?

A: Components are bundled and strapped on wooden pallets. Uprights and beams are packed separately to maximize container loading:

  • A 40HQ container typically holds 25–30 tons of racking components
  • We provide a packing list and loading photos
