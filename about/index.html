About RackingHub

An independent platform built to serve the warehouse racking industry with reliable information, curated resources, and practical tools.

Our Mission

The warehouse racking industry lacks a single, neutral source of information that buyers, planners, and facility managers can trust. Product literature is biased, trade publications are fragmented, and standards documents are difficult to access and interpret.

RackingHub exists to fill that gap. We aggregate, curate, and explain the information that matters most to professionals working with warehouse storage systems.

What We Provide

  • Resource Directory

    A vetted directory of standards, associations, manufacturers, and tools.

  • Knowledge Center

    In-depth guides, standard breakdowns, and technical explainers written by experts.

  • Planning Tools

    Free browser-based tools for warehouse layout and preliminary cost estimation.

  • Expert Consultation

    Connect with industry professionals for project-specific guidance.

Editorial Principles

These principles guide every piece of content and every resource we publish.

Independence

RackingHub is not owned, funded, or controlled by any racking manufacturer or trade association. Our recommendations are based solely on merit and relevance.

Accuracy

All technical content is reviewed against official standards (FEM, RMI, EN, AS) and verified by qualified engineers. We cite our sources and update content when standards change.

Relevance

We only publish resources and content directly relevant to warehouse racking, storage systems, and related logistics infrastructure. No off-topic filler.

Transparency

If a resource listing is sponsored or a partnership exists, we disclose it clearly. Users always know the nature of the content they are viewing.

Accessibility

Technical information should be understandable to procurement managers and facility planners, not just structural engineers. We bridge the gap between engineering and business.
