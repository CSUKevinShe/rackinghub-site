About RackingHub

22+ years of manufacturing excellence behind every racking system we deliver

Who We Are

Boracs
The Manufacturer

Founded in 2004, Boracs is the manufacturing backbone. With a purpose-built facility in Nanjing, Boracs operates fully automated production lines, holds FEM, CE, and ISO9001 certifications, and ships over 50 containers annually to 60+ countries.

→
RackingHub
The Export Platform

Launched in 2025, RackingHub is Boracs' dedicated international sales platform. RackingHub gives global buyers direct access to Boracs' factory — cutting out middlemen, ensuring transparent pricing, and providing English-speaking project support from inquiry to delivery.

One factory, one platform. Every racking system comes directly from the Boracs production line, backed by 22+ years of manufacturing expertise and a track record with brands like IKEA, Qatar Airways, Goodyear, Cotton-On, WHO Lebanon, and the Qatar World Cup 2022.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped our growth

2004

Company Founded — Established in Shanghai as a specialized racking manufacturer

2004 - 2006

ISO9001:2000 Certified — One of the first to ship pre-galvanized frames to Europe

2007 - 2010

AS4084 Compliance — Racking installed in Chile withstood the magnitude 8.8 earthquake in 2010. Supplied IKEA and Goodyear (7,000 stillages)

2010 - 2019

Major Growth — ISO9001:2015, AS4084-2012, FEM 10.2.02 & 10.2.09 certified. Supplied Gurit (UK/Spain/Canada), ASRS projects in Thailand and China. CE Certification obtained

2020 - 2023

Global Expansion — Qatar World Cup 2022 / Qatar Airways project. IKEA & WHO Lebanon. Cotton-On Australia & Singapore. ASRS, AGV, Shuttle Systems for Australia & China

2024

New Factory — Relocated to a modern, expanded manufacturing facility. Racking building system project in Finland. Shuttle racking to UK (>50 x 40HC containers). CTU & AGV racks to Japan. OEM services for Korean manufacturers

2025

RackingHub Launch — Expanded footprint at KOREA MAT 2025, securing factory and distributor partnerships. Launched RackingHub as dedicated export platform for international clients

Our Production Lines

Fully automated manufacturing ensuring consistent quality at scale

⚙ Fully Automated Upright Production Line

  • 32 stages of roll-forming process for highest quality
  • PLC programmable controller with servo motor feeding
  • Automated punching and roll-forming, efficient and quality controlled
  • Infrared length detection cutting ensures installation accuracy

🔨 Fully Automated Beam Production Line

  • 23 stages of high-accuracy roll-forming
  • Automated cutting, flipping, enlapsing and stacking
  • Rotary encoder + servo mechanism for precise cuts
  • Seamlessly integrated conveyor and palletizing system

🔧 Automatic Beam Welding Machine

  • Double-station automatic welding, no work stoppage
  • PLC-controlled welding gun produces seamless, perfect welds
  • PLC-controlled adjustment jig ensures high accuracy
  • High work efficiency with consistent output quality

🎨 Powder Coating Production Line

  • Comprehensive pre-treatment: shot blasting, oil removal, cleaning, phosphating
  • Automatic dual-station spray with Swiss GEMA guns
  • Large cyclone with secondary recovery system for high powder utilization
  • Advanced hot air circulation system for stable curing temperature

Quality Control

We implement first-piece inspection for every component, at every production station, for every order. Additionally, QC inspections are conducted at 30-minute intervals to ensure consistent quality throughout the production process.

We also conduct regular tensile and yield strength tests to ensure only qualified raw materials are used in production.
