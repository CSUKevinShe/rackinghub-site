Who We Are

We are a leading manufacturer and supplier of warehouse storage systems based in Nanjing, China. With over 20 years of industry experience, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and delivering high-quality racking and shelving solutions to clients worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans 50,000 square meters, equipped with advanced production lines and automated coating systems. We serve customers across 60+ countries, from Southeast Asia to Europe, Latin America to Africa.
